AI PING PONG


Instructions

  1. 1.Keep your laptop screen straight
  2. 2.Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop
  3. 3.Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist
  4. 4.Press the play button and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop
  5. 5.The red paddle is your paddle, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist
